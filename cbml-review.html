Overall Review: 10/10 – Can't Buy Me Love is an enjoyable, feel-good romantic comedy that combines humor with heartfelt moments. The series offers a light, entertaining watch with relatable themes about love, identity, and self-worth. It’s charming in its simplicity, providing plenty of sweet moments while exploring the ups and downs of relationships. Though it follows a familiar rom-com structure, it remains engaging thanks to its warm performances and appealing characters. It’s a perfect series for fans of lighthearted romance with a touch of humor and heart.
